    About Us:

    So Why Steller and Not Stellar?

    At Steller Industries, our name is more than just a word—it’s a reflection of who we are and what we do. We chose "Steller" because it sounds like "stellar," representing excellence, but with a unique twist. The "elle" in the middle is a deliberate design choice, symbolizing both aspects of our business: sound (since it sounds like "stellar") and visual creativity (as the spelling is more visually distinctive, just like our photography and videography work). It’s a name that embodies our commitment to delivering top-tier sound, lighting, and visual experiences—because in everything we do, we aim to be truly Steller!

    Your Experience Matters

    Steller Industries, formerly known as Flashpoint Productions, has been at the forefront of event production and technology solutions since 2014. Our journey began with a focus on sound and lighting, ensuring events of all scales had the best audio clarity and visual ambiance. However, as the industry evolved and client needs grew, so did we.

    In early 2024, we embraced a new identity—Steller Industries—to reflect our expanding expertise and commitment to innovation. From our roots in sound and lighting installations, we have broadened our scope to offer comprehensive event solutions, including photography, videography, network installations, and event support services.

    Whether it's a live concert, corporate function, private celebration, or creative production, we provide cutting-edge equipment, professional expertise, and tailored services to bring our clients’ visions to life. At Steller Industries, we are more than just a service provider—we are your event technology partners. With a decade of experience and a passion for excellence, we continue to push boundaries, ensuring every event, installation, or production is executed flawlessly.

    Our Vision

    At Steller Industries, our goal is to become a trusted leader in the event production industry by delivering exceptional service, high-quality equipment, and innovative solutions. We are committed to constantly evolving to meet the growing demands of our clients, ensuring that every event, installation, and project is executed with precision and professionalism.

    Beyond event production, we aim to expand our expertise into new technological fields, including CCTV installations, security video systems, advanced sound and lighting setups, custom computer building, and network infrastructure solutions. By integrating these services, we strive to create seamless, high-performance environments for businesses, venues, and individuals alike.

    As we grow, our focus remains on cutting-edge technology, client satisfaction, and industry excellence, ensuring that Steller Industries continues to set new standards in both event production and technical solutions.
